The PDC organizes year-round events

The Production Designers Gathering

An international conference for 250 production designers, production design professors and students from over 35 countries, set on the island of Spetses, Greece.

International Production Design Week

A week-long celebration of production design with over 200 events in 50 cities around the world, aiming to educate and inspire filmmakers, design professionals and audiences.

PD360

Members-only online platform and app, where colleagues can interact, meet, and access exclusive content.
